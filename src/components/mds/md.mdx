## Overview

Scale-DiT enables **efficient ultra-high-resolution** text-to-image generation by combining:

- **Hierarchical local attention** on high-res latents (fixed-size windows) to reduce attention from quadratic to near-linear complexity
- **Low-resolution global guidance** with scaled positional anchors to preserve global semantics
- A lightweight **LoRA bridge** to keep global structure and local detail consistent during denoising
